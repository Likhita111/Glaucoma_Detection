What is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, which is vital for good vision. This damage is often caused by abnormally high pressure in your eye.

It's one of the leading causes of blindness for people over 60 years old. But blindness from glaucoma can often be prevented with early treatment.

  • Affects peripheral vision first
  • Often has no symptoms in early stages
  • Can lead to permanent vision loss if untreated
  • Regular screening is crucial for early detection
